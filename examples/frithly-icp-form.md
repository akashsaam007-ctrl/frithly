# Frithly ICP Form

Use this when Frithly is selling its own lead-intelligence and outbound research service.

## Recommended inputs

Target Job Titles:
Founder, Co-Founder, CEO, CRO, VP Sales, Head of Sales, RevOps Manager, Head of Revenue Operations

Target Industries:
B2B SaaS, Sales Software, Revenue Intelligence, Sales Enablement, RevOps Software, GTM AI, Prospecting Software

Company Size Min:
20

Company Size Max:
250

Geographies:
US, CA, UK

Trigger Signals to Look For:
Hiring SDRs, Hiring AEs, Hiring RevOps, Hiring demand gen, Recent funding, New GTM content, Expansion into new markets, New pricing page

Hard Exclusions:
Publishers, media companies, communities, job boards, directories, universities, nonprofits, government, local businesses, restaurants, agencies without a software product, consulting firms over 1000 employees, consumer apps, ecommerce brands

Product Description:
Frithly is a B2B lead intelligence and outbound research service for founders, SDR teams, small sales teams, early-stage SaaS companies, and B2B service businesses. We help customers improve outbound prospecting by delivering researched target accounts, relevant decision-maker contacts, company-fit context, buying and intent signals, and personalized outreach angles.

Brand Voice:
direct

## Why this version is better

- It is tighter around software and GTM teams, so the generator is less likely to drift into publishers, job boards, or local-business noise.
- It widens the employee band from the earlier narrow 80-200 range, which should improve volume without making the ICP too loose.
- It adds clearer exclusions for the exact junk classes that were showing up in recent runs.
